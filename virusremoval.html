  • No one program by itself will provide complete coverage and protection. I use a wide range of detection tools and definition dictionaries to scan your system.
  • It is physically impossible for a virus to damage your computer's hardware. There is no need to buy a brand new hard drive and lose all your data, there are very few infections that can't be removed with the right tools.
  • I can install a top of the line security system on your computer or optimize the settings of your current system.
