Virus and Malware Removal

Viruses, spyware, malware, trojans etc. are harmful programs that infect your computer to steal personal data, spam you with unwanted ads and spread themselves to other computers. These viruses often disguise themselves as legitimate looking antivirus programs and can be very hard to detect and remove.
